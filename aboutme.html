About Me

I am a wife and a mother to two wonderful kids. I live Northeast of Atlanta in Hoschton, Georgia. I have wanted to be a programmer since I was in high school. For over ten years I worked from home as a backend web developer. I've taken some time off work to concentrate on training and updating my skills.

My goal is to be able to work from home as a Fullstack Web Developer. I have experience in ASP.NET Web Forms development in C# and VB.NET with a SQL Server backend. I have been updating my frontend skills with HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript. I enjoy all aspects of the web development process from coding the HTML, styling with CSS, making it interactive with Javascript and connecting it to the backend database.
